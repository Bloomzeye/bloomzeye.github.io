Hi hi, I'm Bloomzeye! I'm a Black and Queer, nonbinary indie game developer, and I make cute and colorful pixel art!

about my blog

My goal of this blog is to document and experiemnt with my game dev and art, and grow stronger in my creative and writing skills.

Since I'm ace/demi and a lesbain, I want to start making games about the power of friendship...and lesbains! In due time...allll in due time.

I'm not the best at web development...so this blog is going to be a little wonky as I continue to fix the code...and break something right after. Probably. But hey! Started is better than perfect.

Some of my hobbies are thrifting and park dates with the homies, teaching my younger siblings game dev, and crocheting or other fiber arts.
