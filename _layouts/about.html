Hi hi, I'm Bloomzeye! I'm a Black and Queer, nonbinary indie game developer that goes by They/Them pronouns.

I've been making games ever since I drew that one really bad pixel art bee and turned it into my first published game back in 2021.

I live in the south of the United States in EST timezone, my hobbies are fiber arts, rewatching Mob Psycho for the nth time, playing itch.io games with my younger siblings, thirfting and park-dates with friends, and being a cat-parent!


about my blog

This blog will be where I share my indie game dev and art journey, as I make cute and colorful pixel art and games. I'm strengthing my game/creative writing skills, and I've been known to do many a game jam with friends and my younger siblings!

Since I'm also ace/demi and a lesbain, I want to start making games about the power of friendship...and lesbains! In due time...allll in due time.

I'm not the best at web development...so this blog is going to be a little wonky as I continue to fix the code...and break something right after. Probably. But hey! Started is better than perfect.
