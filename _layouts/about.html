about my blog

My blog here blog is for documenting and experiemnting with my game development, design, art, and grow stronger in my creative and writing skills.

Some of my hobbies are thrifting and park dates with the homies, teaching my younger siblings game dev, crocheting or other fiber arts, and taking naps with my cat-son and cat-nephew.
